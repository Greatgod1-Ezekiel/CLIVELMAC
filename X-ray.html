X-RAY WELDING AND INSPECTION

XRAY WELDING SERVICES

At CLIVELMAC Integrated Service, our X-Ray Welding and Inspection division provides high-precision testing solutions designed to ensure the structural integrity, strength, and reliability of welded joints across industrial projects. We utilize advanced radiographic techniques to detect internal flaws that are invisible to the human eye, allowing us to guarantee that every weld meets strict engineering and safety standards.

Our inspection processes are anchored on internationally recognized codes and standards, ensuring accurate detection of cracks, porosity, inclusions, lack of fusion, and other subsurface imperfections. With certified inspectors and state-of-the-art radiographic equipment, we deliver results that support client decision-making during fabrication, installation, commissioning, and maintenance.

At CLIVEMAC, we understand that quality welding is the backbone of industrial reliability. That is why our radiographic testing is carried out with precision, efficiency, and strict adherence to safety protocols—ensuring that every weld is dependable in the most demanding environments.

Our expertise covers a wide range of sectors, including construction, manufacturing, fabrication yards, mechanical installations, energy facilities, and heavy-duty industrial projects. Whether for new fabrication or maintenance, our X-Ray Inspection services provide the assurance needed to prevent structural failure and operational downtime.

PRECISION, SAFETY & COMPLIANCE

Radiographic testing demands a balance of expert skill and strict safety measures. At CLIVELMAC, we maintain a professional inspection environment governed by global safety regulations for radiography operations. Our technicians follow controlled exposure procedures, shielding protocols, and monitoring systems to protect personnel, equipment, and the environment.

Every inspection is documented with detailed reports, digital radiographic images, defect classification, and compliance certification—giving clients complete confidence in the integrity of their welded assets. With advanced imaging technology and experienced inspectors, we ensure accurate interpretation and reliable assessment of weld quality every time.

OUR XRAY TESTING CAPABILITIES

CLIVELMAC offers a comprehensive range of radiographic and weld-testing services to meet diverse industrial needs. Our capabilities include:

• Digital Radiography (DR) — High-resolution, fast-processing imaging
• Film-Based Radiography — Traditional yet highly reliable weld inspection
• Weld Quality Assessment — Internal defect identification and classification
• Thickness & Density Evaluation — Accurate measurement of material strength
• Structural Integrity Analysis — Ensuring long-term operational safety
• On-site Radiography — Mobile radiographic testing teams for remote projects

With these tools and expertise, CLIVEMAC ensures that every weld meets engineering specifications, performance expectations, and industry regulations.

FUTURE OF NON-DESTRUCTIVE TESTING (NDT)

As industries evolve, so do inspection technologies. CLIVELMAC is preparing for the next generation of Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) by adopting advanced imaging solutions, AI-supported defect analysis, and real-time digital inspection systems. These innovations will allow for faster, safer, and more accurate weld assessments.

Our vision is to remain at the forefront of NDT solutions by investing in training, equipment upgrades, and global-standard quality systems. With this, we continue to deliver inspection services that align with modern industrial demands for reliability, sustainability, and precision.
